# Goat Network RPC Endpoints

To interact with any Ethereum-based network, you'll need reliable RPC providers. Choose from the options below to get started:

- **Alpha Mainnet RPC**: `https://rpc.goat.network`
- **Testnet3 RPC**: `https://rpc.testnet3.goat.network`
- **[dRPC](https://drpc.org/chainlist/goat)**: Your go-to provider for fast and secure RPC access to Goat Network.
- **[Ankr](https://www.ankr.com/rpc/goat/)**: A robust, decentralized RPC service designed to support the Goat ecosystem.
- **Alpha Mainnet Archive Node**: `https://archive.goat.network`

Build an awesome app and apply to the [Founders Club](https://founders.goat.network/) to gain exclusive benefits and grow with our network.
